**01 What is NagpurLens?**

NagpurLens is a city-scale real estate intelligence platform being built
specifically for Nagpur. It integrates government property registration
data, infrastructure development maps, price benchmarks, and demographic
data into a single analytical system.

The platform produces zone-level investment scores, price trend
analysis, and growth hotspot identification --- enabling data-driven
real estate decisions for investors, homebuyers, and researchers.

**02 The Problem We Are Solving**

Nagpur is undergoing one of the fastest urban expansions in central
India --- driven by MIHAN, the metro network, the ring road, and Smart
City investments. Despite this rapid growth, a fundamental gap exists:

-   Individual investors and homebuyers lack access to consolidated,
    > data-backed analysis of which areas are appreciating

    > Property decisions are driven by broker opinions and word-of-mouth
    > rather than evidence

    > No public platform currently integrates registration trends,
    > infrastructure proximity, and price benchmarks for Nagpur

    > Early-stage appreciation zones are identified only after prices
    > have already risen

This information gap leads to missed opportunities for buyers who act
late, and risk for investors who act on incomplete information.

**05 Scope & Market Opportunity**

NagpurLens targets a large and underserved audience in one of India\'s
fastest-growing cities:

-   First-time homebuyers in Nagpur --- a market of hundreds of
    > thousands of families

    > Small investors with ₹20--50 lakh looking at residential plots and
    > agricultural land

    > NRIs from Nagpur wanting data-backed remote investment guidance

    > Real estate brokers and developers seeking data to strengthen
    > client pitches

    > Urban planning researchers, journalists, and policy analysts

The platform is currently being built as a data analytics project.
Future scope includes live data feeds, ML-based price forecasting,
mobile accessibility, and potential API access for brokers.

**06 Why Nagpur, Why Now**

-   MIHAN --- Asia\'s largest integrated multi-modal hub --- is
    > operational and expanding, driving employment and real estate
    > demand around Nagpur airport

    > The Nagpur Metro Phase 1 is complete; Phase 2 is underway,
    > reshaping accessibility across the city

    > The Nagpur Ring Road is opening new development corridors
    > previously considered peripheral

    > Smart City Mission investments are transforming infrastructure
    > across NMC zones

    > Despite all of this activity, no data intelligence platform exists
    > for Nagpur real estate

This is a narrow window. Once prices in growth corridors are widely
recognized, the early-mover advantage disappears. NagpurLens aims to
make that advantage accessible through data.
